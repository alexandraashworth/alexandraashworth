Fashion has one of the largest impacts on the environment. Not only does fast fashion encourage wasteful purchasing of clothes for short periods of time, the materials used are hugely damaging to the environment, even when washed. On this page find three helpful hints to help curate a wardrobe that is not harmful for the environment, and lessens your carbon footprint!

Invest!
Upcycle!
Thrift!

Simple ways to change your habits...

  • Invest in quality pieces from sustainable brands!
  • Make use of clothes you no longer need by using them for other purposes. Alternatively, donate your clothes to charity shops or recycle what cannot be used.
  • Buy clothes from vintage stores or charity shops to reuse pre-loved clothes!

Every little helps!
