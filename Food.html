SO HOW CAN YOU HELP ...

Don't think to make positive food choices you have to become a strict vegan or never buy meat or meat products again. There are small changes you can make to your diet and eating habits that will still have a positive impact on the environment.

  • Try and get more of your protein from plant based sources such as nuts, seeds, lentils and pulses.
  • Not cutting out meat consumption altogether but just reducing your intake, Try starting with one meat free day a week and work upwards from their.
  • Swapping out some animal products from your diet such as switching from cows milk to almond or soya milk.
  • Be aware of the packaging your food is coming in and instead try and support local groceries and food markets, whilst bringing you own reusable bags to reduce food packaging.
