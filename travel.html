Get ready to save the world!

Tip 1: Use Public Transport

If just one person switches from a car to public transport they can reduce their household carbon footprint by 30% (source)

Tip 2: Cycle More

If your jounrney to work is 10 km each way and you switch from driving to cycling you'd reduce greenhouse gas emissions by 1500 kg in a year (source)

Tip 3: Car Share

If just 100 people from your local community carshared every day for a year, 1,320 pounds of carbon monoxide and 2,376,000 pounds of carbon dioxide would be kept from the air (source)

Tip 4: Switch to an Electric Car

On average electric cars produce half the amount of carbon pollution as a convetional car (source)
